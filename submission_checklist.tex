\section*{Pre-submission Checklist}

Authors are requested to ensure that their manuscript adheres to the following guidelines before submission to the \textbf{Nexus AI \& Education (NExAIE)} journal.

\begin{itemize}[label=$\square$]
    \item \textbf{ORCID IDs:} All authors have provided their ORCID IDs and they are correctly linked.
    \item \textbf{Corresponding Author:} At least one corresponding author is clearly identified with an email footnote.
    \item \textbf{Article Type:} The correct category (e.g., RESEARCH ARTICLE, REVIEW ARTICLE) is selected.
    \item \textbf{AI Disclosure:} The PEDAL Archive protocol ID and citation are correctly included in the metadata.
    \item \textbf{Accessibility (WCAG 2.1):} All figures use the \texttt{\textbackslash nexaieFigure} command with descriptive Alt-Text.
    \item \textbf{Graphical Abstract:} A full-width graphical abstract image is provided at the start of the document.
    \item \textbf{Declarations:} All mandatory sections (Funding, Author Contributions, Data Availability, Conflicts) are present.
    \item \textbf{References:} All references follow APA 7th Edition and include mandatory DOIs.
\end{itemize}
