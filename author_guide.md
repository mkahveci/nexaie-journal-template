# NExAIE Author Guide & Technical Writing Specifications

Welcome to the **Nexus AI & Education (NExAIE)** author guide. As an AI-native journal, we prioritize technical precision, accessibility, and transparency. 

**Official Journal Link**: [nexaie.kahveci.pw](https://nexaie.kahveci.pw/index.php/nexaie)


---

## 1. Submission Formats
We strongly encourage submissions via our **Official Overleaf Template**.
*   **Primary Format**: LaTeX (using `nexaie.cls`).
*   **Alternative Format**: Microsoft Word (`.docx`) is accepted only if it adheres strictly to the specifications below.

---

## 2. Technical Specifications

### 2.1 Typography & Layout
*   **Main Font**: Times New Roman (or equivalent LaTeX `newtxtext`).
*   **Monospaced Font**: Courier or Consolas (for code and AI Prompt IDs).
*   **Line Spacing**: Single-spaced for final layout; double-spaced for review manuscripts.
*   **Columns**: Standard submissions use a two-column layout. Review versions should use a single-column, line-numbered format.

### 2.2 Section Hierarchy
1.  **Title**: Bold, 18pt, centered.
2.  **Authors & Affiliations**: Followed by ORCID iDs and Corresponding Author footnotes.
3.  **Abstract**: Maximum 250 words, single paragraph.
4.  **Keywords**: 4–6 descriptive terms.
5.  **Headings**:
    *   Level 1: **BOLD ALL CAPS**
    *   Level 2: **Bold Title Case**
    *   Level 3: *Italic Title Case*

---

## 3. AI Disclosure & Transparency (The PEDAL Protocol)
NExAIE requires full disclosure of AI involvement in the research or writing process.
*   **AI Protocol Citation**: Authors must cite the specific AI Review or Prompt Protocol used, ideally hosted on the [PEDAL Archive](https://kahveci.pw/pedal-archive/).
*   **Citation Format**: `Protocol: PEDAL-ID/vX (Author, Year)`.
*   **Verification**: All AI-assisted components must be verified by a Human Expert or Section Editor.

---

## 4. Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 / ADA)
To ensure inclusivity, all submissions must meet the following standards:
*   **Figure Alt-Text**: Every image must include an "ActualText" or "Alt-Text" description (max 150 words).
*   **Color Contrast**: Minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for text in figures.
*   **Non-Color Cues**: Use line styles, patterns, or shapes in charts—never rely solely on color.
*   **Simple Tables**: Avoid merged cells (`multirow/multicolumn`) to ensure screen reader compatibility.

---

## 5. References & Citations
*   **Style**: APA 7th Edition.
*   **DOI**: Mandatory for all references where available.
*   **BibTeX**: Authors using LaTeX must provide a `.bib` file using the `biber` backend.

---

## 6. Mandatory Declarations
Every manuscript must include the following sections before the references:
*   **Funding**: Acknowledge grants and institutional support.
*   **Author Contributions**: Use the CRediT (Contributor Roles Taxonomy) format.
*   **Data Availability Statement**: Links to repositories or instructions for access.
*   **Conflicts of Interest**: Explicit declaration of any competing interests.
